Division of labour: analysts own weights, targets, theses and entry/exit strategy (holdings file); dealers own executions (trades file); market data — price, market cap, valuation, momentum, quality — is fetched by the app, not typed.
In this mock the signal feed is a simulated snapshot. In production a Vercel cron hits yfinance (.NS tickers) nightly for prices/momentum and FactSet for fundamentals, writes z-scores vs the Nifty 500 cross-section to Postgres, and every upload is versioned with an audit trail.
